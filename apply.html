NOTICE: sinθ magazine has open positions!

Sine Theta Magazine is looking to expand its editorial team!

Please note that all of these roles are volunteer — at this time, none of the magazine’s staff are paid. These roles are not meant to substitute paid jobs. All levels of staff work unpaid on the magazine as a passion project alongside their other responsibilities. Nonetheless, applicants will ideally seek to prioritise Sine Theta as a passion project.

We are looking for:

Print design intern

Duties include:

  • Provide graphic design support to the Art Director and design staff as needed for promotional material (e.g. posters, social media graphics, etc.).
  • Use Adobe InDesign (and other supplemental Adobe Suite software as needed) to participate in the design process of each quarterly print issue, laying out pages in creative ways that reflect the spirit and ‘vibe’ of short stories, poetry, and nonfiction features included in the magazine.
  • Help brainstorm and potentially help create merch items.
  • This position falls under the supervision of the Managing Editor.

Desired Qualities:

  • Timeliness and proactiveness. We expect that this role will take on average a few hours per week. Work is especially important around the release dates for our quarterly production cycle and can be more low-key during the weeks before we enter into production of the next issue of the magazine.
  • Open and effective communication.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Cooperative mindset.
  • Background in graphic design.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite with emphasis on InDesign.
Publicity intern

Duties include:

  • Proactively seek out new opportunities for Sine Theta related to stockists or events
  • Create marketing materials and conduct email outreach campaigns under the supervision of the managing editor
  • Monitor prize and award nomination opportunities, as well as potential grant, sponsorship, and funding offerings that pertain to the magazine
  • Assist with publication-wide application copy
  • Help organize community Discord and general Zoom events
  • This position falls under the supervision of the Managing Editor.

Desired Qualities:

  • Timeliness and proactiveness. We expect that this role will take up to a few hours per week.
  • Open and effective communication and strong writing skills.
  • Flexible schedule.
  • Willingness to both learn and offer original ideas.
  • Ability to handle multiple projects at once.
  • Fluency in English is mandatory.
  • Background in event programming and/or grant-writing a plus, but not mandatory
Editorial Intern

Duties include:

  • Read and edit submissions for print publication.
  • Help source interviewees for print issues.
  • Contribute to discussions on editorial direction.
  • This position falls under the supervision of the Editor-in-Chief.

Desired Qualities:

  • Timeliness and proactiveness. We expect that this role will take up to a few hours per week.
  • Open and effective communication and strong writing skills.
  • Flexible schedule.
  • Willingness to both learn and offer original ideas.
  • Ability to handle multiple projects at once.
  • Fluency in English is mandatory.

Applications will be considered until September 15, 2022 on a rolling basis. Each position requires a commitment of a minimum 3 month period.

Each position has the potential to become permanent, or upgraded from the “intern”-level to a full staff role if editors are satisfied with interns’ attitude and performance. There is also a possibility that the intern position may be renewed beyond the minimum 3 month period.

Applicants should send an email to sinethetamag@gmail.com with a brief cover letter in the body of the email (we recommend 300-500 words as a non-binding guideline) indicating: 1) the position you are applying for, 2) any relevant past experience to the role you would like to include, and 3) your personal reasons and motivations for applying to the position.

Moreover:

  • If you are applying for the Print design intern position, please provide us with one or more samples of your previous graphic design work. You do not need a formal portfolio/portfolio site to apply; any several print design-related samples of your work are helpful. You also do not need any formal design education to apply; we are happy to review applications from self-taught designers. Once you have applied, we will also send you a design exercise as part of the application process.
  • If you are applying for the Editorial intern position, please provide us with a short writing sample (~100-300 words for prose, at least 10 lines for poetry). Excerpts of longer works are permitted (please contextualize the fact that it is an excerpt with no more than one sentence about the work), and you can submit either prose or poetry, previously published or unpublished. We will not use the sample that you submit for anything other than the purposes of the application process. Once you have applied, we will send you a brief editorial exercise as part of the application process.
  • If you are applying for the publicity intern position, please note that we will follow up with a brief publicity exercise as part of the application process.

All applications will receive acknowledgement of receipt. If your application has not been acknowledged by 3 days after applications close, it might have gone to spam: please send a separate email enquiry, and if that goes unacknowledged, please contact us on social media.

Applicants who pass the first round of applications will be invited to interview with one or more members of the editorial board who are familiar with the position’s responsibilities.

Sino creators worldwide with experience relevant to the open positions are encouraged to apply. We are especially excited to work with creatives who are based outside of the United States. Simultaneous applications for multiple positions are welcome.

We look forward to receiving your applications and welcoming you into the Sine Theta family!
