NOTICE: sinθ magazine has open positions!

Sine Theta Magazine is looking to expand its staff!

Please note that all of these roles are volunteer — at this time, none of the magazine’s staff are paid. These roles are not meant to substitute paid jobs. All of our staff work unpaid on the magazine as a passion project alongside their other responsibilities. Nonetheless, applicants will ideally seek to prioritize Sine Theta as an important commitment.

We are looking for:

Web content intern (estimated 8 hours per week; workload remains consistent weekly)

Duties include:

  • Provide content-writing and research support to the Web Editor and other members of Sine Theta staff as needed for our social media features of artists/writers.
  • Assist with event management as needed.
  • Actively manage (daily) the Sine Theta Patreon Discord community, including virtual events and ideas for regular engagement.
  • Design simple graphics to promote material from our archives.
  • Help manage social media accounts.
  • Assist with writing promotional copy for issue releases.
  • Assist with proofreading, formatting, and preparing print magazine interviews for uploading online.
  • Help conceptualize and execute our annual Patreon drive. In 2024, this will run November 8–24.
  • This role is overseen by the Web Editor.

Desired Qualities:

  • Open and effective communication.
  • Strong writing skills.
  • Familiarity with social media, particularly Instagram and Twitter.
  • Prior experience with using Discord is desired. Learning Discord on the job is acceptable, but the applicant must understand that this role requires active maintenance of our Discord server.
  • Timeliness and proactiveness. This work ordinarily involves checking in several days per week over small-scale, discrete tasks, and communicating with an internationally-based team.
  • Collaborative attitude. Willingness to both learn and offer original ideas.
  • Fluency in English is mandatory.
  • Knowledge of and translation skills of the Chinese language is a plus.
Web and publicity intern (estimated 8 hours per week; workload remains consistent weekly)

Duties include:

  • Assist with remaining updated on past print issue contributors and interviewees.
  • Proactively seek out new opportunities for Sine Theta related to stockists and events.
  • Create marketing materials and conduct email outreach campaigns.
  • Monitor prize and award nomination opportunities, as well as potential grant, sponsorship, and funding offerings that pertain to the magazine.
  • Track upcoming publications/releases of past interviewees and contributors for us to share and celebrate.
  • Assist with drafting grant application copy.
  • Help organize community Discord and Zoom events.
  • Help conceptualize and execute our annual Patreon drive. In 2024, this will run November 8–24.
  • This role is overseen by the Publicity Editor, working closely with the Web and Editorial teams.

Desired Qualities:

  • Open and effective communication.
  • Strong writing skills.
  • Familiarity with social media, particularly Instagram and Twitter.
  • Prior experience with using Discord is desired. Learning Discord on the job is acceptable, but the applicant must understand that this role requires active maintenance of our Discord server.
  • Prior experience writing grant applications or working within an organization’s development team is a plus, but not required.
  • Timeliness and proactiveness. This work ordinarily involves checking in several days per week over small-scale, discrete tasks, and communicating with an internationally-based team.
  • Collaborative attitude. Willingness to both learn and offer original ideas.
  • Fluency in English is mandatory.
  • Knowledge of and translation skills of the Chinese language is a plus.

Applications will be considered until September 20, 2024 on a rolling basis. Each position requires a commitment of a minimum 3 month period.

Each position has the potential to become permanent, i.e. upgraded from the probationary “intern”-level to a full staff role if editors are satisfied with interns’ attitude and performance. There is also a possibility that the intern position may be renewed for further 3-month periods.

Applicants should email sinethetamag@gmail.com with a brief cover letter in the body of the email (we recommend 300-500 words as a non-binding guideline) indicating: 1) the position you are applying for, 2) any relevant past experience to the role you would like to include, and 3) your personal reasons and motivations for applying to the position.

Supplemental materials:
  • Web content interns: please provide us with two sample written posts about any Sino creative or piece of Sino-created art or writing of your choosing, in the style of our Instagram features (please familiarize yourself with them first: instagram.com/sinethetamag). Your posts should indicate an awareness and knowledge of effective communication and audience optimization on Instagram. Please attach this as a separate Word document to your email; include any associated images with the “Instagram post” in the Word doc alongside the caption text.
  • Web/publicity interns: Provide brief answers (100 words or less) to the following prompts: 1) Explain why Sine Theta should receive a grant funding opportunity to support our work; 2) propose a project that Sine Theta could undertake using grant funding.

Where suitable, web content intern applicants will also be considered for web/publicity intern roles, and vice versa.

All applications will receive acknowledgement of receipt. If your application has not been acknowledged by 3 days after the deadline, it might have gone to spam: please send a separate email enquiry, and if that goes unacknowledged, please contact us on social media.

Applicants who pass the first round of applications will be invited to interview with one or more members of the editorial board who are familiar with the position’s responsibilities.

Sino creators worldwide with interest relevant to the open positions are encouraged to apply. We are especially excited to work with creatives who are based outside the United States.

We look forward to receiving your applications and welcoming you into the Sine Theta family!
