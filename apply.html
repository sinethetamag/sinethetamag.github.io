NOTICE: Sine Theta Magazine is hiring!

Sine Theta Magazine is looking to expand its editorial team! To keep pace with the growth of our publication, we are looking for:

Web content intern

Duties include:

  • Provide content-writing and research support to the Web Editor and other members of Sine Theta staff as needed for the daily Sino arts blog and magazine issues.
  • Write at least two posts a week for the Sino Daily Arts blog.
  • Help manage social media accounts.

Desired Qualities:

  • Open and effective communication and strong writing skills.
  • Skill with social media, including but not limited to Tumblr, Instagram, Facebook, and Medium.
  • Timeliness and proactiveness.
  • Willingness to both learn and offer original ideas.
  • Fluency in English is mandatory.
  • Knowledge of and translation skills of the Chinese language is a plus.

Print design intern

Duties include:

  • Provide graphic design support to the Art Director and other members of Sine Theta staff as needed for the quarterly print issue as well as the magazine’s online presence

Desired Qualities:

  • Open and effective communication.
  • Timeliness and proactiveness.
  • Cooperative mindset.
  • Background in graphic design.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite with emphasis on Indesign.

Editorial intern (Role begins in October)

Duties include:

  • Commission and edit submissions for print issues
  • Copyedit print issues prior to publication
  • Help source interviewees for print issues
  • Contribute to discussions on editorial direction
  • Write at least one post a week for the Sino Daily Arts Blog
  • Represent Sine Theta locally and seek opportunities to promote and expand the magazine.

Desired Qualities:

  • Open and effective communication and strong writing skills.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Timeliness and proactiveness.
  • Flexible schedule.
  • Willingness to both learn and offer original ideas.
  • Ability to handle multiple projects at once.
  • Fluency in English is mandatory. Knowledge of and translation skills of the Chinese language is a plus.

How to apply:

Applications will be considered until August 31, 2019 on a rolling basis. Each position requires a commitment of a minimum 3 month period. Each position has the potential to become permanent if editors are impressed with interns’ attitude and performance.

Applicants should send an email to sinethetamag@gmail.com with a 1-page cover letter indicating the position you are applying for, any relevant past experience to the role you would like to include, and your personal reasons and motivations for applying to the position. Additionally,

  • If you are applying for the Web content intern position, please provide us with two sample written posts about any Sino creative or piece of art from a Sino creative art of your choosing, in the style of our blog posts. Posts can either be in the formatting of a Tumblr post or an Instagram post, but your posts should indicate an awareness and knowledge of the social medium you choose.
  • If you are applying for the Print design intern position, please provide us with one or more samples of your previous graphic design work. Once you have applied, we will send you a design exercise.
  • If you are applying for the Editorial intern position, please provide us with a short writing sample (~100-300 words). Once you have applied, we will send you an editorial exercise.

Sino creators worldwide with experience relevant to the open positions are encouraged to apply. Applications for multiple positions are welcome. We look forward to receiving your applications and extending the Sine Theta family!
