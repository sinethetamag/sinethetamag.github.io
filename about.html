ABOUT THE MAG

sinθ (Sine Theta) is a print-based magazine created by an international cohort that aims to connect and empower members of the Sino diaspora like ourselves. Our content is centered around creative expression and celebration of our identity.

The magazine was founded in March 2016 by Jiaqi Kang, Iris Lang, Elisabeth Siegel, and Michelle Tay.

sinθ magazine is grateful for the support of its patrons, without which it would not be here today.

With thanks to: Arron Luo, Alina Abidi, Anny, Gabriel Bailey, Kiana Borjian, Stephanie Chang, Giorno Giovanna, George Iskander, Joyce, Kevin Lu, Laura Wang, Ethan Yu, and Qianze Zhang.
