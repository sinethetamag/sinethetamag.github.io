ISSUE #18 "MORNING 朝"

朝不保夕。。。at dawn, unsure what evening brings; a precarious, contingent state.

Submissions to Sine Theta Magazine's 18th issue, themed “MORNING 朝”, are officially open! Thanks to our Patreon subscribers, as of this issue, Sine Theta Magazine is a paying publication - all contributors will be paid $10 USD. Read our new submission guidelines for more details.

We want works which taste like dew before dawn. Pieces of art which crack open, bringing with it the surprise of newly made eggshell or the first light. Can you make works that feel like wide water, without sound? Can your writing startle, the way a pun does? This issue asks for submissions that are interested in possibility and mouths, in the rising action before the arc of a narrative is determined.

Submissions are due SUNDAY, JANUARY 3, 11:59 PM PDT to sinethetamag@gmail.com for full consideration of your work(s). Visit our submission guidelines. Feel free to DM or email us with any questions you may have; we welcome pitches ahead of time!
