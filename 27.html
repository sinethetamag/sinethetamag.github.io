ISSUE #27 “FRIEND 朋”

三朋四友。。。Friendships of all kinds, tree branches reaching for one another.

Submissions to Sine Theta Magazine's 27th issue, themed “FRIEND 朋” are officially open! Thanks to our Patreon subscribers, Sine Theta Magazine is a paying publication—all contributors will be paid $10 USD per piece.

In “FRIEND 朋,” we want to weave a tapestry documenting the seasons of friendship: the initial spark and the ending fizzle and the tender moments that fall in-between. We want to celebrate community and connection. Swim in the rivers of your childhood memories. Ponder companionship and blood feuds, family gatherings and secret networks, all-consuming crushes and adolescent jealousies. Pull apart the relationships that shape all of us into who we are. Collaborative works are highly encouraged.

Submissions are due SUNDAY, 26 MARCH, at 11:59 PM PST to sinethetamag@gmail.com for full consideration of your work(s). Visit our submissions guidelines for more information. Feel free to DM or email us with any questions you may have; we welcome pitches sent ahead of the deadline!
